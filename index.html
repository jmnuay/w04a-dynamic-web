Women with Baskets

Short Introduction about Anita Magsaysay-Ho

Anita Magsaysay-Ho was one of the most celebrated Filipino modernist painters and the only female member of the renowned Thirteen Moderns group. Her artworks are known for depicting the strength and grace of Filipino women, often portraying them performing everyday rural activities such as weaving, harvesting, and carrying baskets. Her painting "Women with Baskets" highlights the harmony, dignity, and collaborative spirit of Filipino women.
